Our Signature

The Famous Raan

Slow-roasted on open wood fire, marinated overnight in 18 secret spices. A whole leg of glory — Pune's most talked-about dish.

🐑
⭐ House Special · Must Try
Mutton RaanWhole Slow-Roasted Leg
🌶️🌶️🌶️🌶️

Extra Spicy

A full leg of farm-fresh mutton marinated for 24 hours in our secret blend of 18 village spices — raw papaya, Kashmiri chilli, ginger-garlic, and aromatic garam masala. Slow-roasted over charcoal until the meat falls off the bone. Served with tandoor roti, green chutney, and fresh onions.

₹1,800Feeds 3–4 people
Pre-book

🔥 Bestseller · Family Favourite
Chicken RaanWhole Leg, Village Style
🌶️🌶️🌶️

Medium–Hot

A whole chicken leg, marinated in turmeric, red chilli, coriander, and our special village masala paste. Roasted slowly over wood fire until the skin is perfectly charred and the meat inside is juicy and flavourful. Lighter than mutton but just as unforgettable — and a hit with kids too!

₹750Feeds 1–2 people

Kids Zone & Entertainment

While the adults enjoy the Raan, the kids have a blast! We've created a magical world of fun, adventure, and laughter — right here at Kesnand Village.

🌟 Star Attraction

Horse Riding

Safe, guided horse riding for children of all ages. Our trained horses and experienced handlers make it a joyful first-time experience kids will never forget.

🎪 Live!
🤡

Cartoon Characters

Live visits from favourite cartoon characters on weekends — Chota Bheem, Doraemon, Mickey Mouse, and more! Perfect for photos and surprises.

🎠

Merry-Go-Round

A classic colourful merry-go-round that keeps the little ones spinning with joy. Safe and supervised for toddlers and young children.

🧸

Toy Train Ride

A fun mini train ride through the village grounds — kids absolutely love it! Runs every 30 minutes on weekends and holidays.

🏹

Archery for Kids

Introduction to archery with soft-tip arrows and colourful targets. Builds focus and gives kids a real sense of achievement.

🎨

Art & Face Painting

Kids get to express themselves with face painting and simple art activities — a great creative break between the outdoor adventures.

🌾

Farm & Nature Walk

A guided walk through the village farm — kids get to see and touch animals, learn about vegetables, and connect with nature.

🎮

Indoor Game Zone

Air hockey, carom, table tennis, and board games — for when it's too sunny outside or just for some friendly family competition!
